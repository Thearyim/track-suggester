Track Suggester

Have you ever wondered what kind of developer you are?

The software development landscape changes constantly. There isn't an official industry glossary of terms. Understanding the skills that each type of developer needs to have is confusing to newcomers, and can be intimidating to non-technical people. Wouldn't it be nice to have quiz to determine the type of developer will fits you most? Answer the following questions and see for yourself!

Does the idea of working in a cloud computing architecture interest you?

Do you imagine yourself doing mobile device development (e.g. Android, IOS devices)?

Do you like the idea of being the face of the business?

Do you get more excited about the look of a Ferrari than the engine inside?

Does the idea of working with complex systems of hybrid technologies excite you?

Do you really enjoy designing the way application look?
